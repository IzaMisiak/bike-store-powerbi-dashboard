**Project Overview**

This project presents an interactive Power BI dashboard built using the Bike Store dataset. The report provides a comprehensive analysis of sales performance, product profitability, and customer behavior to support data-driven business decisions.

The dashboard consists of four report pages:
- Landing Page
- Sales Overview
- Product Overview
- Customer Overview

**Business Objectives**

The main goals of the report are:
- Monitor overall sales performance and growth trends
- Identify the most profitable products and product categories
- Analyze customer demographics and purchasing behavior
- Evaluate regional sales performance
- Support strategic decision-making through KPI tracking

**Tools & Technologies**

- Power Query
- Power BI Desktop
- DAX
- Data Modeling

**Dashboard Pages**

- **Landing Page**

  Interactive navigation page providing quick access to all report sections.

- **Sales Overview**

  Key metrics and sales performance analysis:
  - Total Sales Revenue
  - Average Profit
  - Average Order Value
  - Sales Growth Rate
  - Sales by Product Category
  - Revenue Trend Analysis
  - Revenue and Profit Comparison
  - Most Profitable Products
    
- **Product Overview**

  Product performance analysis including:
  - Margin Percentage
  - Profit per Unit
  - Total Orders
  - Top 10 Products by Profit
  - Product Category Distribution
  - Product Performance Analysis
  - Sales Revenue and Average Unit Price by Product Category
  - Monthly Quantity Sold vs Target

- **Customer Overview**

  Customer and geographical analysis:
  - Average Customer Age
  - Total Female Customers
  - Total Male Customers
  - Most Profitable Products by Region
  - Profit by Geography
  - Average Order Value Analysis

**Key Insights**

- Bikes generate the highest sales revenue among all product categories.
- Sales revenue shows consistent growth over the analyzed period.
- Product profitability varies significantly across categories and individual products.
- Customer purchasing behavior differs by demographic group and geographic region.
- California represents one of the strongest-performing markets in terms of sales and profitability.
